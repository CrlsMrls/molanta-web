About us

LaMolanta starts with the dream to bring closer the catering to our clients, to offer nutritional products, healthier and delicious, in a closer way and comfortable, where the product excellence has the leading role.

We want to minimize the footprint our restaurant has in the environment, we are very careful with the paper, glass and plastic recycling. Using recyclable and biodegradable packaging. We do vermicompost decomposing vegetable and food waste, with the created bedding materials we feed our plants, that recognize it with flowers and leaves.

We are a project based on people. Overall, we believe our team is the real strength of our project.
